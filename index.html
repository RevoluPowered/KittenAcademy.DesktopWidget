Live Cat Viewer - Kitten Academy

A live stream viewer created for viewing the kitten academy live-stream. I created this because I was really enjoying the kitten academy live stream, additionally I can't have cats in my apartment; this really is the next best thing.

This software was made in a very short period of time, expect bugs, report them.

Should you just want a link to their stream, watch here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YPv9yKC76hE
